Industries We Support

Polymer solutions for automotive, compounding, packaging, industrial, and electronics applications.

Automotive Plastics

We supply polymer additives and materials for interior and exterior automotive components, under-the-hood applications, and structural parts. Our coupling agents and impact modifiers help meet demanding mechanical, thermal, and aesthetic requirements while supporting lightweighting and cost targets.

Related materials: Coupling agents, impact modifiers, engineering masterbatch, glass fibers, flame retardants.

Plastic Compounding

Compounders rely on consistent, high-quality additives and raw materials for filled and reinforced compounds. We provide coupling agents, impact modifiers, pigments, and specialty additives that integrate smoothly into your formulations and support consistent output quality.

Related materials: Coupling agents, impact modifiers, commodity and engineering masterbatch, pigments, recycled polymer materials.

Packaging

Packaging manufacturers need reliable color, process aids, and functional additives for films, containers, and rigid packaging. We supply masterbatches and additives that support clarity, color consistency, and processing efficiency for flexible and rigid packaging applications.

Related materials: Commodity masterbatch, pigments, polyolefin elastomers, recycled polymer materials.

Industrial Manufacturing

From machinery components to construction products and industrial equipment, we support manufacturers with polymer additives that enhance strength, durability, and processability. Our portfolio covers reinforcement, impact modification, and color solutions for a wide range of industrial applications.

Related materials: Coupling agents, impact modifiers, glass fibers, engineering masterbatch, flame retardants.

Electronics Components

Electrical and electronics applications demand materials that meet safety, thermal, and dimensional stability requirements. We supply flame retardants, engineering masterbatches, and specialty additives for connectors, housings, and insulating components.

Related materials: Flame retardants, engineering masterbatch, coupling agents, impact modifiers.
