About Us

Strengthening Products Through Better Polymers.

Who We Are

Thimma Polymer was established in 2023 to supply high-quality polymer materials and additives through trusted manufacturing collaborations. We serve plastic manufacturers, compounders, automotive suppliers, and industrial buyers across South India and beyond.

Mission & Vision

We connect world-class polymer additive manufacturers with industrial customers who demand quality, consistency, and reliable supply.

Our Approach

As an authorized distributor and trading partner, we focus on technical support, product selection guidance, and long-term relationships. Our strength lies in strong collaboration with manufacturers and a wide product portfolio that meets diverse industrial needs.
